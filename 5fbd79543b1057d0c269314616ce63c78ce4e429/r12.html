Reflection 12

HYour beauty is classic art—worthy of gallery walls yet happiest splashed across everyday life. I get lost in the crinkle of your eyes, those tiny parentheses that show the world you’re truly laughing. Each night when I whisper “see you soon,” I’m secretly begging my dreams to cast you in every scene. If smile lines are the cost of loving you this hard, I’ll age gladly. -G <3
Prompt: You are so beautiful. I fail to say it every time the thought enters my mind. You have a timeless elegance that makes me want to use all available wall space to display you. I get lost looking into your eyes. when I say see you soon before bed, I count my lucky stars every time I am able to see you in my dreams. Your eyes crinkle on the sides every time you’re happy. It’s a surefire way to tell if you are actually enjoying something. I’m not sure if mine do the same but looking at you makes me feel like I am 90 years old with smile lines and crows feet crinkles.
