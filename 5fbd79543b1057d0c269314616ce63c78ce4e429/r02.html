Manchester trains, three-minute sprints, and a too-confident Uber driver—every frantic moment felt perfect because you were beside me. A bargain “meal deal” became a feast, fish-and-chips tasted like a Michelin plate, and dancing in the dark toward that midnight burger turned the whole city into our jukebox. The loud nights thrill me, but it’s the quiet exhale in between that makes my heart click back into place. Thank you for choosing to run, stroll, and sometimes tumble through life with me. -G <3
